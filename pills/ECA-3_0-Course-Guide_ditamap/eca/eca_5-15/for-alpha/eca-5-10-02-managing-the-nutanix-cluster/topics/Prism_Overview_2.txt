Nutanix Prism Element is an end-to-end management solution for an AHV
virtualized datacenter.

Nutanix Prism provides central access to configure, monitor, and manage
virtual environments in a simple and elegant way. Prism offers
simplicity by combining several aspects of datacenter management into a
single, easy-to-use solution.
